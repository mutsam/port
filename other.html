Document Preparation for Container Receipt

Importance of Document Preparation

  • Ensures smooth customs clearance.
  • Avoids delays and additional costs.
  • Guarantees accurate verification of goods.

Failure to prepare the required documents can lead to:

  • Delays in customs processing.
  • Additional storage fees at the port.
  • Potential fines or penalties.

Required Documents

  • Bill of Lading (Original)
  • Commercial Invoice
  • Packing List
  • Certificate of Origin
  • Power of Attorney
